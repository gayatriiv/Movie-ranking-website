Best Movies According to Me

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Eternal sunshine of spotless mind

One of the great movies I’ve ever seen. The way it beautifully intertwined love and science fiction was simply magical. The editing, smoothly transitioning from new to old memories, was masterfully done. The score added a profound layer and Jim Carrey’s serious performance was exceptional. What strikes me is, despite its incredible qualities, why isn’t this film more popular?

Me Before You

It’s an emotional tearjerker with plenty of laughs and palpable chemistry between the two leads!

Charlie

excites those who love the unusual
